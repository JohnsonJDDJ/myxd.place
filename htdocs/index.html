Los Angeles and Palm Springs 2022

2022 July

Experience the power of the California desert.

Immediately after 四天疲惫的搬家过程, we began our trip to Los Angeles and a 2h drive city nearby called Palm Springs. "It's only two hours away from Los Angeles, so the weather must be similar." Turns out we are just wrong.

The moment we stepped out of our car at the Indian Canyon, we know we are 寄了. We didn't even mind to check the weather, so Mikee is still thinking "没事的，成都现在比这里还热", not knowing that it is already 43 degree Celsius.

Everthing is good apart from the temperature. Tourist attractions at Palm Springs are basically empty apart from the Aerial Tramway. The 360 degree rotating Tramway was a great surprise, but nothing compares to the cool breeze at the top of Mt San Jacinto. A Zoo Keeper at the Palm Springs Zoo told us we are brave to be visiting at this time of the year. We agreed. Even at the evening, when we are having a walk in the hotel after sunset, it still feels that we are inside a huge sauna.

Mikee's friend caught COVID just one day before we arrive at Los Angeles, so we are going to visit Mike together. Mike is eager to take us to play some golf, which we are hesitant at first. We went to Malibu and 瞬间后悔. Stuck in traffic and no parking, we drove back and went to play golf at last. Golf turns out to be very fun, and 李想是个很好的老师.

The Lego Treehouse

2022 May

Our first Lego project to kick off summer.

Our's, and also Mikee's, first Lego project. After a long struggle to choose between the Treehouse and the Typewriter, we think that the Treehouse is more aesthetically pleasing (even though it might cause great trouble when we move).

With the accompaniment of《向往的生活》and《乘风破浪的姐姐》, building the Treehouse felt like a breeze. 曾鸣麒巨急, suggests that we should build simultaneously. But clearly the Treehouse is unable to be built simultaneously. So we divided our role to be builders and finders, but we constantly want to take over other's responsibility. 只能说，好胜心很强。

We are quite amazed by the details in this Treehouse. The hidden treasure, the treasure map on the bunk bed...But Johnson's favorite thing to do is to 折磨坐在秋千上的人。

NorCal Road Trip 2022

2022 March

830 miles in a ZipCar.

It is actually our first spring break at the states even though we are already juniors. In the midst of oil price inflation, using a ZipCar for a road trip sounds very attractive. Since we have already gone south from Berkeley, this time we will head north.

At Point Reyes, the wind were so strong. When we are back in our car, 我们脸麻又耳鸣. When we resided in Santa Rosa, we discovered that the Airbnb we booked is only a single room in a house.

At Mendocino, we had a very great lunch at a local resturant. We discovered that we had mistakenly brought the keys (Santa Rosa's Airbnb) with us, so we need to ship it back. The walk at the Mendocino headlands are wonderful, 我们看见了一只很胖的狗.

At Eureka, In-N-Out便宜的惊人！We spent both nights at our stay enjoying junk food and《甄嬛传》. The size of the trunks in the Redwood National Park is amazing. Johnson ruined his shoes as he tried to climb a waterfall.

At Sacramento, we decided to stay in a RV for some 新鲜感. We lit a pathetically small campfire and Johnson taught Mikee the 100% win strategy for tic-tac-toe.

California-1 Road Trip 2022

2022 Jan

Praises to the great California Coastline.

虽然已经开学了, but we are lucky to have friends coming all the way from New York to spend their remaining holiday with us. We decided to rent an house and a car to have a last bit of fun during a what may seems to be a very busy semester.

经典夜游三番活动不能少, including viewing the Golden Gate Bridge at Battery Spencer. As we get out of the car, 隔壁车里的Emo小哥默默点起了一根烟, probably because we are a group of two couples, and he is all alone by himself.

Our trip on California-1 highway was way over expectation. We wrote down our team name "你好肚脐" while exploring the caves at sharkfin shore. The color of the beach at Pfeiffer's beach was not purple at all. Sunset at the Bixby Bridge was the absolute highlight of the trip.

We spent the nights at the house watching American Horror Stories and playing board games such as 骷髅牌. 我们在海底捞玩的“叠菠菜”, which is exactly the sort of things we might do when we are still back in China, the good old "COVID holiday".

New York 2022

2022 Jan

Late Christmas in New York.

In hindsight, we were extremely lucky during this trip. We went to Times Square of New York during the Omicron outbreak, which is the worst COVID surge ever. There were countless people at Times Square without mask on, but somehow we didn't caught COVID.

Andy offered his place for us to stay. We really have forgotten how convenient it is to live in a proper city. The market on the opposite side of the street contained everything that have ever existed on the shelves in Berkeley, but only with even more variants. Mikee had her first 川麻 experience. We enjoyed hotpot with 奶茶冰粉.

The city was covered in snow when we woke up the second morning. Fearlessly, I volunteered to join one of the street shows in Times Square. Johnson cried when watching The Phantom Of the Opera. New York is very cold during the winter.

2021 Fall Miscellaneous

2021 Fall

Fifth semester as Golden Bears.

September 4th, 2021. Johnson bought roses from Trader Joe's, picked out 3, brought the remaining 9 and went to see Mikee. Everything is very cliche, and everything happened very quick. It is a cute simple ceremony, 不花哨，不做作。We think its ok.

The begining of the semester was especially hard for Johnson. Symptoms were re-emerging, which is a very bad sign since we are away from our family and our usual treatment procedure. But luckily, Johnosn has Mikee. One step at a time, Johnson found the right clinic and treatment. The symptoms are pretty much gone at the end of the semester.

一个学期，说长不长，说短不短。For us, we believe it is just a begining. We feel ready and confident to face more that is to come. This website will be the place to rest our memories. But sometimes, memories aren't a thing to be written down. The subtle ones are to be remembered only, deep in our heads, safe and secure.
