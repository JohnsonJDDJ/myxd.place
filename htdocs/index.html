The Lego Treehouse

2022 May

Our first Lego project to kick off summer.

Our's, and also Mikee's, first Lego project. After a long struggle to choose between the Treehouse and the Typewriter, we think that the Treehouse is more aesthetically pleasing (even though it might cause great trouble when we move).

With the accompaniment of《向往的生活》and《乘风破浪的姐姐》, building the Treehouse felt like a breeze. 曾鸣麒巨急, suggests that we should build simultaneously. But clearly the Treehouse is unable to be built simultaneously. So we divided our role to be builders and finders, but we constantly want to take over other's responsibility. 只能说，好胜心很强。

We are quite amazed by the details in this Treehouse. The hidden treasure, the treasure map on the bunk bed...But Johnson's favorite thing to do is to 折磨坐在秋千上的人。
