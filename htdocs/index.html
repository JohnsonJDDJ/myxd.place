The Lego Treehouse

2022 May

Our first Lego project to kick off summer.

Our's, and also Mikee's, first Lego project. After a long struggle to choose between the Treehouse and the Typewriter, we think that the Treehouse is more aesthetically pleasing (even though it might cause great trouble when we move).

With the accompaniment of《向往的生活》and《乘风破浪的姐姐》, building the Treehouse felt like a breeze. 曾鸣麒巨急, suggests that we should build simultaneously. But clearly the Treehouse is unable to be built simultaneously. So we divided our role to be builders and finders, but we constantly want to take over other's responsibility. 只能说，好胜心很强。

We are quite amazed by the details in this Treehouse. The hidden treasure, the treasure map on the bunk bed...But Johnson's favorite thing to do is to 折磨坐在秋千上的人。

NorCal Road Trip 2022

2022 March

830 miles in a ZipCar.

It is actually our first spring break at the states even though we are already juniors. In the midst of oil price inflation, using a ZipCar for a road trip sounds very attractive. Since we have already gone south from Berkeley, this time we will head north.

At Point Reyes, the wind were so strong. When we are back in our car, 我们脸麻又耳鸣. When we resided in Santa Rosa, we discovered that the Airbnb we booked is only a single room in a house.

At Mendocino, we had a very great lunch at a local resturant. We discovered that we had mistakenly brought the keys (Santa Rosa's Airbnb) with us, so we need to ship it back. The walk at the Mendocino headlands are wonderful, 我们看见了一只很胖的狗.

At Eureka, In-N-Out便宜的惊人！We spent both nights at our stay enjoying junk food and《甄嬛传》. The size of the trunks in the Redwood National Park is amazing. Johnson ruined his shoes as he tried to climb a waterfall.

At Sacramento, we decided to stay in a RV for some 新鲜感. We lit a pathetically small campfire and Johnson taught Mikee the 100% win strategy for tic-tac-toe.
